About Me

Hello, I'm Kelly Alcinay

Reliable, ethical Licensed Practical Nurse with about 9 years of extensive experience in the delivery of patient-centered and quality health care. Endowed with vast idea in providing daily living and personal care services to critically ill, disabled or cognitively impaired patients in accordance with an established care plan. Driven with the keen interest of saving live through proven skills in prioritizing medical care and problem-solving. Physically strong with a high-level of patience and effective communication skills as well as excellent documentation, time-management, and management skills.
